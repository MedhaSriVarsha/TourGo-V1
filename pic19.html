Dolphin's Nose – Visakhapatnam

Dolphin's Nose is a dramatic rocky promontory in Visakhapatnam that juts boldly into the Bay of Bengal, shaped remarkably like a dolphin's nose. Rising approximately 174 meters above sea level, it offers breathtaking panoramic views of the deep-blue sea, the bustling harbour, and the sprawling city below.

A historic lighthouse perched atop the headland has guided seafarers for generations and remains visible from many kilometres away. The combination of natural grandeur, maritime heritage, and stunning coastal vistas makes Dolphin's Nose one of Vizag's most iconic and unforgettable attractions.

🕐 Open: 9 AM – 5 PM
🎟️ Entry: Nominal
